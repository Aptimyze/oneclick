General Questions

What is 1-Click?

1-Click enables you to quickly and easily identify the most appropriate options for making a trip by consolidating information about all of the transportation services available in your area to present trip itineraries tailored to you.

How do I change the language of the application?

You can change the language by clicking on the language links in the lower left hand corner of the application. If you are a registered user, you can also set a default language on your travel profile.

Where can I go for more information about the 1-Click initiative?

The home page contains information and links to additional 1-Click information.

How can I get more help with the use of 1-Click?

Contact the 1-Click team via email.

If you are a registered user, you can also get assistance planning trips and managing your account.

Trip Planning

The Trip Planning workflow (Trip → Options → Review → Plan) allows you to plan a trip including from and to location, dates/times, transportation modes, eligibility and required accommodations. Based on the entered information, potential trip itineraries are displayed allowing you to select the most appropriate.

Trip

The "Trip" planning page is where you enter the details of the trip that you want to take. For example, where you are going, when you are going, what modes of transportation you would consider taking.

What transportation modes can I plan trips using?

1-Click plans trips using the following modes of transportation:

  • Bike
  • Drive
  • Paratransit: demand-response and volunteer services
  • Rideshare: Carpool and vanpool options
  • Taxi: Local taxi services
  • Transit: local regular schedule bus and rail services
  • Walk

Why do I need to give the purpose of my trip?

Some services only provide trips for certain purposes, such as medical visits or grocery trips. Giving the purpose of your trip allows 1-Click to determine whether you are eligible for those trip itineraries.

How do I set the from and to locations of my trip?

The from and to locations can be defined by typing an address, a place of interest or a saved place and selecting the appropriate item from the list. Alternatively, you can use the flag tool to select a location on the map.

I have a place that I will be traveling to/from frequently. Is there a way for me to save it?

If you register for an account, you can save your frequently used places for easy reuse.

Options

Some trip itineraries may only be available if you meet certain eligibility criteria (e.g., age, disability status, trip type), and there may be certain accommodations (e.g., door-to-door service, need to travel with a folding wheelchair) you need. The Options page within the Trip Planning workflow allows you to define (or not) your eligibility and your required accommodations.

Why was the Options page not shown as part of the workflow?

The Options page is only displayed if you requested Paratransit as a transportation mode on the Trip page.

Why do I need to answer these questions?

The questions help determine your eligibility for certain specialized services, and ask about any accommodations that you might need. Your answers are used to determine the Paratransit services meet your needs.

What happens if I don't answer the questions?

If you do not answer the eligibility questions on this page and 1-Click finds a trip option with a Paratransit service that has eligibility requirements, you will be required to answer any relevant questions before you are able to select that trip.

If you do not answer questions about necessary accommodations, 1-Click will not be able to tell you if a service might be unable to meet your needs.

Review

Based on the entered trip information, multiple trip itineraries are displayed for the outbound and return (if return trip requested) legs of the trip.

How can I see more details about a displayed trip itinerary?

To see a summary of the trip itinerary, on a desktop computer hover the mouse point over the displayed trip itinerary colored bar or on a touchscreen device tap the bar.

To see the full details of the trip itinerary, on a desktop computer click the displayed trip itinerary colored bar or on a touchscreen device double-tap the bar.

I only want to see certain kinds of trips--how can I hide the ones that I'm not interested in?

On the left side of the Review page, you can filter the trip itineraries by mode of transportation, number of transfers, trip cost and trip time.

How do I select the outbound and return trip itineraries?

Click the "Select" button to the right of the chosen outbound and return trip itineraries.

Why do some trip itineraries have question marks instead of Select buttons?

If a Paratransit service used for a specific trip itinerary has eligibility requirements and you have not answered the relevant questions either on the Options page or on your travel profile (for registered users), you will have to answer those questions by clicking on the "?" button to the right of the trip itinerary before you can proceed with selecting that trip itinerary.

What do I do if my trip returns no itineraries/ no itineraries that will work for me?

You can use the "Feedback" button in the upper right of the page to send feedback about trips that don't turn up any satisfactory itineraries.

I want to change something about my trip or my answers to the questions. How can I do this?

Under the logo in the upper left of the page, there are breadcrumb links that will take you back to each step in the trip planning process allowing you to make edits and replan your trip.

Plan

The Plan page displays the details of the selected trip, allowing you to print or email the details.

I planned a trip that requires booking in advance. How do I do this?

Trips can be booked by contacting the service"s provider.

How can I save this trip so that I can plan it again?

When you register for an account with 1-Click, all trips that you plan are automatically saved to "My Trips", accessible when you log in.

User Account

Why should I register for a user account?

While you can plan a trip using 1-Click without creating a user account, registering gives you access to additional features including:

  • Designating users and agencies who can help you plan trips
  • Saving information about your eligibility and travel needs
  • Reusing trips you have already taken
  • Saving frequently used locations

How do I register for a user account?

You can register for a user account using the "Create an Account" link found by clicking the "Welcome, Visitor" menu.

My Travel Profile

Your travel profile stores your contact details, preferred language and transportation modes, buddies and authorized agencies, and the answers you have provided to the eligibility and accommodation questions,

How do I change the information on my travel profile?

You can change your travel profile information by clicking the "Edit" button in the upper right corner of the page. Don't forget to apply your changes when you are done editing.

Why do I need to answer Eligibility and Accommodations questions?

This section allows you to save your answers to the questions asked on the Trip Planning "Options" page.

What are agencies and why would I approve one on my profile?

Agencies are organizations with staff who can assist you in planning trips or managing your 1-Click user account. You must add an agency on your travel profile for its agents to be able to assist you.

How can I revoke an agency"s permission to see my information and plan trips for me?

You can revoke an agency"s privileges at any time by editing your travel profile and revoking their buddy status in the "Agencies" section.

What is a buddy?

A buddy is a friend, family member or other caregiver who is allowed to plan trips on your behalf or on whose behalf you are allowed to plan trips.

How can I make someone my buddy?

Both you and your buddy must have 1-Click user accounts. If this person is not already registered with 1-Click, ask them to create an account.

If you want someone to be able to plan trips for you, you can add them as a buddy when you are editing your travel profile. Use the "Add" button that appears in the "Buddies" section while you are editing. Enter the email address that your buddy used to register for 1-Click. A buddy request will be sent. Be sure to save your profile changes.

If someone wants you to plan trips for them, ask them to send you a buddy request. When editing your travel profile, you can accept (or request) that request in the "Buddies" section next to their name.

How can I revoke a buddy"s ability to plan trips for me?

You can revoke a buddy"s privileges at any time by editing your profile and revoking their buddy status in the "Buddies" section.

My Trips

 All trips that you plan are saved to your user account.

How do I view my trips?

When logged in as a registered user, you can see your past and scheduled trips by clicking the "My Trips" link in the "Welcome" menu.

How can I replan a trip that I have taken?

After a trip has been taken, you can use the "Replan" button in the "Actions" column to replan that trip. Don't forget to reset the dates/times for the trip.

How can I provide feedback about a trip I have taken?

You can provide feedback about a trip you have taken, the service you travelled with, or an agency that assisted you by clicking the "Feedback" button next to the trip you would like to comment on.

Why can't I see the feedback that I just left?

All feedback needs to be approved before it shows up on the 1-Click site.

My Places

You can store regular trip origins (e.g., your home) and destinations (e.g., your workplace, your hospital) in "My Places".

How are my saved places used?

When you are planning a trip, you can start typing the name of one of your saved places. Selecting the name will automatically load the saved address.

How do I view my places?

When logged in as a registered user, you can see your saved locations by clicking the "My Trips" link in the "Welcome" menu.

How do I save a new place?

When viewing your "My Places" page, you can add a new place by entering the address, selecting the correct option from the list of addresses that appears below the field, and then entering a name for your new place. When you are done, click "Save". The place then appears in your "My Places" list and on the map.

Assistance

How can I get assistance using the application?

If you are having difficulty with the application, contact one of these local agencies. An agent can help you book a trip and/or set up an account. If you already have an account, you can give any of these agencies permission to plan trips on your behalf.

As a registered user, you can also ask other register users to help plan trips for you.
